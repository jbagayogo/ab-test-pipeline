## Define Over-performance 
Consider what it would mean if the treatment dramatically exceeded expectations.

Over-performance could mean that employees in the treatment group spent significantly more time engaging with the material than anticipated, which could indicate the redesigned slides were genuinely more compelling — or alternatively, that they were harder to follow and required more time to process. It would be worth asking: 

Is there a point at which viewing time becomes too long, suggesting confusion rather than engagement? 

Were there any external factors during the treatment period, such as a slower workday or a more motivated group of employees, that could have inflated results?
